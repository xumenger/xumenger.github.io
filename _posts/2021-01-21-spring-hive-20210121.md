首先添加Maven 依赖

```xml
<dependency>
	<groupId>org.apache.hive</groupId>
	<artifactId>hive-jdbc</artifactId>
	<version>2.3.3</version>
	<exclusions>
		<exclusion>
			<groupId>org.eclipse.jetty.aggregate</groupId>
			<artifactId>*</artifactId>
		</exclusion>
	</exclusions>
</dependency>
```
